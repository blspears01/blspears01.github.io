Introduction

At the start of the quarter, I decided to make the topic of my Passion Project based around driving. I received my driving permit in July, and I slowed down on getting my hours completed when school started. By making this my Passion Project, I figured it would help motivate me to get back on track and to continue at a more consistent pace. After about 7 weeks in the quarter, I decided to include coding as part of it. I still had to find a way to present my original Passion Project about driving, so I thought that creating a wesbite would be an interesting way to show it.



Time and Effort

I would estimate that I spent about 25 hours total on this project, when considering the driving time, videos I watched to learn about coding, and the actual amount of time I spent creating this website. I wish I had come up with the idea to include coding sooner because I would have gone into more advanced features if I had more time. I also didn't realize how much I enjoyed doing it. Although the website looks pretty simple and unprofessional it took a TON of code to build.

Reflection

Overall, I am very content with my project. I had a lot of fun coding and driving, although the driving got really stressful at times. I learned a lot about both of these skills throught the 10 weeks. I became much more familiar with programming languages and the necessary components that make up a good website. I'm hoping that this new skill will lead to more advanced skills in the field of computer science or programming. In the future, I will probably try coding games or apps with more sophisticated languages. In addition, to the coding and driving, I have become better at problem solving and handling stressful situations. I am very content with the final product and impact. I go into more detail about these things in the links you can click at the top.
